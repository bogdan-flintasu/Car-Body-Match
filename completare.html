How often will you use the car?
  • If you plan on using the car for your daily activities (going to work or to the gym) you should choose "daily".
  • If you plan on using the car just for the pleasure of driving in the weekend you should choose "weekendly". If you choose this answer you might want to get your attention to a sportier car.

     




Maximum number of people you will be carrying:
  • The number of people you carry in your car determines the capacity of your car, as well as the size. For example, if you need to carry at least 5 people you will need more space and a bigger trunk.If you need your car just for yourself or to use it in the city you should choose "5<".

     




Where will you drive the car?
  • If you want to drive the car in the traffic of your city you might want to choose "urban".
  • If you want to use the car mostly on the motorway you should choose "extra urban". People who travel the country a lot might want to choose this answer.

     




Do you need an extra safe car?

Of course everyone does, but if you choose "yes" you believe safety is the turning point when it comes to making a choice of what car should you buy. You should take it into consideration if you need a car for you and your family.
